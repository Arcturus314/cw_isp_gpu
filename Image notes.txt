RGB: 3-wide, additive color mixing
    sRGB, AdobeRGB, ProPhoto RGB, CIE RGB

RGBA: RGB with additional color channel for transparency
    Used in PNG images

CMYK: 4-wide, subtractive color mixing (printing purposes)

YPbPr: form of analog color space, used in analog systems

YCbCr: form of digital color space
